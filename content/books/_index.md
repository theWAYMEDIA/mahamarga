## Essential Mahamarga Literature

## Featured Text

### [MahaMarga Primary Teaching](/primary-teaching/)
*Sanskrit-English Critical Edition — 128 Chapters*

The foundational Wayist text rendered into classical Sanskrit for scholars, translators, and the permanent record. Covers the complete philosophical framework: the nature of existence, the purpose of human life, the laws governing soul evolution, and the practical path of spiritual development.

This is the authoritative reference text for all future translations of the Mahamarga teaching into other languages.

~ *Salvar Dàosenglu (Wayist collective)*

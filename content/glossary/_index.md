## Sanskrit & Wayist Terminology

Mahamarga uses Sanskrit terms with precise philosophical meanings, often differing from their use in other traditions. This glossary defines the key terms as they are used in the MahaMarga Primary Teaching.

---

## Core Sanskrit Terms

### जीवः (jīvaḥ) — Soul
The mortal, learning being; the caterpillar stage of the hybrid human. The soul does *not* transform and become the spirit — the spirit *emerges* (प्रकटते) as the soul completes its development. Collapsing jīva and ātman into one (as Advaitic readings do) dissolves the soul-school framework entirely.

### आत्मा (ātmā) — Spirit
The immortal spiritual being that emerges from the soul through the developmental process. Distinct from the soul throughout; not the same as Brahman or Universal Self.

### महामार्गः (mahāmārgaḥ) — The Great Path
The universal path of soul evolution. Literally "great road." The Sanskrit name for what English calls Wayism.

### विकासः (vikāsaḥ) — Development / Unfolding
Spiritual development as intentional unfolding, not Darwinian evolution. Used throughout the corpus for "soul evolution."

### अविच्छिन्न-सम्बन्धः (avicchinna-sambandhaḥ) — Unbroken Connection
The Wayist teaching of interconnection between beings — not Advaitic *ekatā* (oneness/merger) but a relationship of unbroken connection while preserving the distinct identity of each being.

### दिव्य-पाठशाला (divya-pāṭhaśālā) — School of Divinity
Earth as purposeful educational institution for souls. Souls are students; the curriculum is karma; graduation is birth in Sukhāvatī.

### सुखावती (sukhāvatī) — Heaven Realm
The Pure Land of Bliss — the spirit realm for our type of consciousness, where graduated souls are reborn as immortal spirit-beings.

### तारा (tārā) — Divine Tara / Spirit Guide
"They who ferry us from one shore to the next." Immortal spirit-beings assigned to guide each soul through the Butterfly Path.

---

## Key Distinctions

### विकासः vs क्रमविकासः
*vikāsa* (development/unfolding) is used for spiritual development, not *kramaviāsa* (sequential evolution) or Darwinian *evolution*. The soul's development is intentional, curriculum-based, and directed toward a specific destination.

### प्रकटते vs भवति
"The spirit *emerges*" (प्रकटते, *prakaṭate*) not "becomes" (भवति, *bhavati*). The caterpillar does not become the butterfly — the butterfly emerges from the caterpillar's dissolution.

### सम्बन्धः vs एकता
*sambandha* (connection, relationship) over *ekatā* (oneness/unity). Wayism teaches deep interconnection, not Advaitic merger where individual identity dissolves into universal Self.
